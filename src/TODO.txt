1 User Authentication & Roles
-Customers and admins can log in and log out.
-Role-based access: customers can see only their accounts. Admins can see/manage all users/accounts.

2 Account Management
-Create accounts (savings, checking, student).
-View account details(balance, account type, account holder information)
-Update account details (contact info).

3 Transaction Management
-Deposit(add money), withdraw(remove money), transfer money.
-View transaction history( Viewing a detailed record of past transactions for a specific account)

4 Customer Dashboard
-Personalized info for each customer (displaying key information like recent transactions, account balances, and quick links to common features)
